MySite module of ABCD helps you to get you user account status

In the first part you get your General User Information such as the ID, Name, Cathegory and the expiration date of your account. If there are any wrong data please contact the Library staff to correct the problem.

In the second part you will see your current:
Active Suspensions:
You will get the list of suspensions you have. You are not allow to reserve,renewal or have any more loans until your suspensions are due. You may talk to a library supervisor if you think this is incorrect.

Current Loans:
The loans that you have are displayed. If you click in the link in the title column you will get the document title in a new row. You may by clicking in the button. If all the requirements are fulfil the system will allow to do a loan renewal. Otherwise you will get a message from the system pointing the problem.

Current Reservations:
The list of reservations on documents you have will be displayed. If you click in the link in the title column you will get the document title in a new row. You may a reservation you won't need.

Fee Account:
You will get the list of pending fines you have. You are not allow to reserve,renewal or have any more loans until you paid your fines. You may talk to a library supervisor if you think this is incorrect.
